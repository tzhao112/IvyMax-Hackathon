Mystery - Genre of fiction that usually involves a murder by an unknown killer or some other crime to be solved

Fantasy - Genre that features magical and supernatural elements that do not exist in the real world.

Non-Fiction and History - A broad genre of books that is 99% true and has happened in the real world

Biographies - A detailed description of a person's life. that portrays a person's experience of these life events

Sports - Genre with info about sports, how they’re played, as well as autobiographies, or biographies of famous players or coaches of popular sports.
